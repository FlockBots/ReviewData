Welcome

Thank you for visiting this website, as that probably means you are interested in helping me improve ReviewBot.

FAQ

How can I help?

ReviewBot analyzes a comment and tries to determine whether it is a whisky review or not. In order to do so it needs to know what a review vaguely looks like. The more comments he has seen that are reviews the better. Unfortunately gathering all exact comment reviews is quite the chore. With this website you can help me out by just analyzing a few comments and clicking a button to tell the bot whether it is a review or not.

Why do I need to login with my Reddit account?

Just to make sure anyone helping me out is doing so in good faith, I like to keep track of your username. It also makes it easier to delete faulty analyzed comments, in case of someone thinks he/she is clever. As the login page shows, I won't get your password or any other data besides your username.

Okay, that seems fair. Where do I start?

Once you have logged in, you should see your username in orange. That's the link to the classification page. Once you arrive there, you will be served a fresh comment. Click one of the two buttons (whichever is appropriate) and you'll get the next one. You can stop whenever you like, of course.

Hint: Press y to classify the comment as a review or n to classify it as a comment instead of clicking the buttons!

Clicking buttons is boring. Can I help you out in some other way?

Of course you can! Using ReviewBot a lot and keeping me up to date with any bugs that might occur is very important. I rarely ever notice the bugs myself once the bot has gone live.

Another way is to contribute to the bot yourself. If you know Python you can help me write the code over on GitHub.
